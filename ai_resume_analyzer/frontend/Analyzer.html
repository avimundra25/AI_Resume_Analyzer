How do I analyze my resume?▼
Upload a PDF or DOCX, type your target job role, click Analyze Now. Results appear in ~30 seconds.
What file formats work?▼
PDF and DOCX up to 5 MB. Ensure your PDF has selectable text (not a scanned image).
How is my score calculated?▼
AI evaluates Content Quality, Skills Match, Structure & Format, and ATS Compatibility — averaged into a score out of 100.
Is my data stored online?▼
No. Resume text is used only for the AI analysis and never stored on any server. Reports live in your browser only.
What is ATS?▼
Applicant Tracking System — software employers use to filter resumes. High ATS score = better keyword alignment.
How do I improve my score?▼
Follow the Suggestions, add Missing Skills, quantify achievements, and match keywords to the role.
