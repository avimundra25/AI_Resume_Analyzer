How do I analyze my resume?▼
Go to Analyzer, upload a PDF or DOCX, type your job role, click Analyze Now.
Where are my reports saved?▼
Reports are stored in your browser's local storage — on this device only.
Is my data stored online?▼
No. Resume text is used only for the AI analysis and never stored on any server.
How do I export a report?▼
Click "Print / Export PDF" inside any report to open your browser's print dialog, then save as PDF.
How do I share a report?▼
Click the "Share" button inside a report. It copies a link to your clipboard that anyone can open.
