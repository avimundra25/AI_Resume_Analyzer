How do I analyze my resume?▼
Upload a PDF or DOCX on the Analyzer page, type your target job role, and click Analyze Now. Results appear in ~30 seconds.
What file formats work?▼
PDF and DOCX files up to 5 MB. Ensure your PDF has selectable text (not a scanned image).
How is my score calculated?▼
The AI evaluates Content Quality, Skills Match, Structure & Format, and ATS Compatibility — averaged into a score out of 100.
Is my data stored online?▼
No. Your resume text is only used during analysis and never stored on any server. Reports are saved in your browser only.
What is ATS?▼
Applicant Tracking System — software employers use to filter resumes before a human reads them. High ATS score = better keyword alignment.
How do I improve my score?▼
Follow the Suggestions and add the Missing Skills from your report. Quantify achievements and tailor keywords to the job role.
Can I share my report?▼
Yes — open a saved report and click the Share button to copy a shareable link. Anyone with the link can view the report in their browser.
